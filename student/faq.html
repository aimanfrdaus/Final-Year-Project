1. How do i book a room?

You can click at the "Book Hostel" on the left sidebar. Then, fill in the informations needed. After you have done filling all the informations, click submit button.

2. I want to change my phone number, how can I do it?

At the top right corner, click on you name then choose "My Profile". There, you can change your phone number and click "Make changes" button.

3. Can I change my password?

Yes, you can change your password by clicking the "account setting" at the top right. Then enter your current password and your new password and click submit.
